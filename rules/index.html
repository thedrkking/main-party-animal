Format & Rules

Tournament
Format

32 Players. 8 Teams of 4 Players.

Round Robin Format

$100,000 in prizes

Teams will compete in a best-of-1 game against every other team in a round robin tournament format.

Each game in each round will consist of 2 teams of 4 players where they will play 1 game of 4 vs 4 in the selected game mode for the specific round.

After 7 rounds, the total wins for each team will be tallied and the rankings/prize pool will be determined.
